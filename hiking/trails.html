Trails

Tubbs Hill Trail

Tubbs Hill is a nice and short 2 mile hike in Coeur d'Alene. Great for beginners or those just looking for a simple, pretty hike. It typically takes less than an hour, and it loops around back to where you started so there's no backtracking, making each sight a new one!

Table Rock

This hike in Boise is only slightly more difficult than Tubbs Hill. It takes about an hour to reach the top, where you get a great view of the city of Boise sprawling beneath you! Make sure to bring a hat for shade and plenty of water though.

Iron Creek Stanley Lake Trail to Sawtooth Lake

This 10-mile trail in the Sawtooth National Forest is great for a longer day hike, taking around 5 hours to get up to Sawtooth Lake and back. Though the terrain is a bit more challenging, the mountain views are worth it! The best time to hike this trail is in the summer and early autumn.

Goldbug Hot Springs

Goldbug Hot Springs is a beautiful hike near Salmon that is about 2 hours round trip, but considering the challenging steep portion right before the hot spring, why not bring a bathing suit so you can enjoy the plethora of hot and cold pools to relax you before heading back?
