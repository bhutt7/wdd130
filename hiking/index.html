Welcome

Are you looking for a new adventure? Do you want to get out and more in touch with nature? Do you just need a break from your troubles? Hiking Idaho Trails has the solution you might be looking for! HIT strives to provide you with the best hiking experience you can find in Idaho, whether you are a seasoned hiker or a beginner looking for a great new activity.

Destress

Hiking is a great way to get away from our busy modern lives. You get to explore nature, enjoy the scenery of our beautiful planet, and with a bit of exercise to boot!

Our Goal

Our goal is to present the best trails for you. We have a number of popular trail suggestions to guide you to your first or next hiking experience. We also provide you with information of what you need to bring to make your hiking experience as simple and enjoyable as possible!
